About What is this thing?

For our ECE4760 (Microcontrollers) Final Project at Cornell University we decided to create a sous-vide cooker. A sous-vide cooker is a type of immersion cooker, where food is placed in airtight bags and kept underwater for an extended period of time.

  • Figure 1

Look at that delicious piece of steak! This was cooked using a sous-vide cooker. As you can see, the inside is perfectly rare like any rare cooked steak, but the outside is also perfectly rare! To give the steak a brown texture, it was seared after cooking. Sous-vide cooking also seals in the moisture of the meat while cooking, leading to juicy perfectly cooked steaks.
